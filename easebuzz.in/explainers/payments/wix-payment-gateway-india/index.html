How to integrate payment gateway in Wix

To integrate the payment gateway into your Wix website, for example, the Easebuzz payment gateway, you will need to:

Step 1: Create an Easebuzz account

To create an Easebuzz account, go to the Easebuzz website and click on the “Sign Up” button. Enter your business details and create a password. Once you have created an account, you must verify your email address and phone number.

Step 2: Create a Wix account (if you don’t already have one)

To create a Wix account, go to the Wix website and click on the “Create a new account” button. Enter your email address and create a password. Once you have created an account, you must verify your email address.

Step 3: Install the Easebuzz Wix plugin

To install the Easebuzz Wix plugin, go to the Wix App Market and search "Easebuzz." Click the "Add to Site" button and follow the instructions to install the plugin.

Step 4: Configure the plugin settings

Once the plugin is installed, you must configure the settings. Go to your Wix dashboard and click the "Manage Apps" button to do this. Click the "Easebuzz" app and then the "Settings" tab.

Enter your Easebuzz Merchant ID and Merchant Secret Key. You can find this information in your Easebuzz account dashboard.

You will also need to select the payment methods you want to accept. Easebuzz supports various payment methods, including credit and debit cards, net banking, wallets, and UPI.

Step 5: Add a payment button to your Wix website

Once you configure the plugin settings, you can add a payment button to your Wix website. To do this, go to your Wix website editor and drag and drop a “Payment Button” element onto your page.

In the payment button settings, select the Easebuzz payment gateway as the payment processor. You can also customize the appearance of the payment button.

Once you have added a payment button to your website, you can start accepting customer payments. Customers who click the payment button will be taken to the Easebuzz website to complete the transaction.

Additional points to consider for integrating a payment gateway into your Wix website:

  • Make sure that you have the latest version of the Easebuzz plugin installed.

  • Test the payment gateway thoroughly before you launch your website.

  • Make sure that your website is PCI compliant. You must use the latest security technologies to protect your customers’ payment information.

  • You can also use the Easebuzz API to integrate the payment gateway into your Wix website. This gives you more flexibility in customizing the payment process.

If you have any questions or need assistance integrating the Easebuzz payment gateway into your Wix website, please get in touch with Easebuzz support.
