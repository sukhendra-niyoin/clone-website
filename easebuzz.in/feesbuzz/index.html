Fees management suite for education business

FeesBuzz is a comprehensive payment solution platform for educational institutes be it schools, colleges, universities or coaching centres. Institutes can manage entire fee collection life cycle batch-wise, course-wise with FeesBuzz and offer 100+ payment modes including credit card, debit card, 50+ net banking options, UPI, Wallets along with EMI options to students.

Schools can create fee installments, semester-wise & batch-wise. Installment once paid is reflected on the dashboard corresponding to each student.

Student fee discount for any particular installment can be easily created by selecting course and batch. It also comes with an expiry date for fees submission.

FeesBuzz offers customised report i.e., transaction-wise, batch-wise, installment-wise, etc. Fee defaults can also be viewed to manage revenue leakages.

Payout details with bifurcation across service charge, service tax, refund can be easily extracted. Institute can also get monthly details of each payout id.

Institutes can create customized forms, configure required student details and also set the properties of each data field as mandatory or optional.

  • Automate fee collection

    FeesBuzz automates split payments to vendor accounts such as hostel, canteen, library, etc.

  • Robust dashboard

    Customised reports with paid and unpaid installments, online & offline fee collections of the students are available on the dashboard.
