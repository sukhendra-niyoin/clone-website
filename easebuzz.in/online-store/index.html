Frequently Asked Questions

How does an online store work?

Create an online store with Easebuzz and add product information, market product using our marketing tools, receive and manage orders from customers, get paid instantly with Easebuzz.

Can I create an online store without the help of a website developer?

Yes, you can create an online store without the help of a website developer.

How to integrate payments on the online store?

We allow you to create products like event tickets,physical items,digital items or subscription based service with free integrated payment gateway.

Do you offer loyalty points or reward points features?

When you create an online store through Easebuzz, you can create customized coupons for loyal customers to retain them.

Why should I create an online store?

Online stores allow shoppers to peruse products and make buying decisions at any time, day or night. Having an online store gives you more channels to engage with customers.
