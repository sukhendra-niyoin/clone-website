Frequently Asked Questions

What types of payment methods does Easebuzz support?

Easebuzz supports a wide variety of payment methods, including credit and debit cards, net banking, UPI, and mobile wallets. Our platform also supports multiple payment modes, making it easy for businesses to accept payments from customers.

Can I customise the billing options for my customers?

Yes, Easebuzz offers a variety of billing options to suit the needs of your business. You can choose from recurring billing, usage-based billing, and one-time payments. Our platform also allows you to set up free trials and introductory pricing to help attract new customers.

How does Easebuzz ensure the security of my customer data?

Easebuzz takes data security very seriously. Our platform uses the latest encryption technologies to protect your customer data, and we are fully compliant with all relevant data security regulations.

Does Easebuzz offer reporting and analytics tools?

Yes, Easebuzz provides comprehensive reporting and analytics tools to help you track your subscription-based revenue. Our platform offers real-time reporting on key metrics like churn rate, customer lifetime value, and revenue per user.

Can I integrate Easebuzz with other software systems?

Yes, Easebuzz seamlessly integrates with a variety of other software systems, including CRM platforms, accounting software, and more. Our platform provides APIs and webhooks to make integration easy and straightforward.

What kind of support does Easebuzz offer?

Easebuzz provides dedicated support to all of our customers. Our support team is available 24/7 to help you with any questions or issues you may have, and we offer a variety of support channels, including email and phone.
