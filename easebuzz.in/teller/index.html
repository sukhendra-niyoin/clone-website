About Teller

Teller is a pay-in and payout product where businesses can have an omnipresent view of their receivables and payables together.

Businesses within the supply chain driven model face difficulties in terms of reconciliation due to tiered approach in terms of distribution network. All such businesses can onboard Easebuzz, use our invoicing solution for collections and make payouts to their vendors/manufacturers from the same place.

  • Drop manual compliance processes and generate multiple e-invoices with GST Numbers in seconds.

  • Increase Cash Flow by regulating purchase-to-pay cycles.

  • Eliminate the time, energy, and stress of submitting invoices & tracking payments manually.

  • One stop solution for all book-keeping and accounting needs.

  • Accurate, timely, scalable and cost effective accounting and bookkeeping B2B solution.

  • No website required. Customers can swiftly pay their invoices online on a webpage safely generated by our payment platform.

  • Manage all invoicing operations through a single user friendly console.

  • End-to-end billing lifecycle management without compromising on security. Teller comes with built-in security features.

Teller at glance

This product is your book-keeping solution in place where distributor or sub-distributor can avail solutions for both collections and payables from one single place. Businesses which are supply chain driven can face problems as follows -

Difficulty in managing multiple sub-distributor network

Mis-management of receivables from retailer to distributor and then from distributor to manufacturer

Revenue leakages in managing payouts to the end manufacturer

High credit period

Frequently Asked Questions

What is e-Invoicing?

E-invoicing is a common B2B practice, integrated solution that enables a fully automated flow directly from one company’s ERP system to another.

How does Electronic invoicing benefit businesses?

Using an E-Invoice saves you a significant amount of money compared to paper and digital invoices. By using a digital invoice, you will no longer need to pay for the paper that your invoices are printed on. It can be automated to send out recurring bills, and can be paid by your customer with just a click of their mouse.

How is Teller better than any other normal B2B invoicing solution?

Teller is a one stop solution for all book-keeping and accounting needs, customers can swiftly pay their invoices online on a webpage safely generated by our payment platform.

Can we track and record offline payments with Teller?

Yes you can track all online and offline transactions.

Can I create and send e-Invoices in bulk?

Yes, e- invoices can be created and sent in bulk.

How do I create an e-Invoice with Teller?

Teller offers seamless integrations for enterprises to generate e-Invoices

Is there any limit to the number of invoices that can be created and tracked?

You can create and track “n” number of invoices through Teller.

Is it safe to manage invoices through Teller?

Yes, it is safe to manage invoices through Teller as it is PCI DSS compliant.
