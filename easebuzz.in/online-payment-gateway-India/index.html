Frequently Asked Questions

What is payment gateway?

A payment gateway is an API used by businesses to accept payments online through different payment modes like credit cards, debit cards, UPI, ewallets, netbanking, etc. Businesses integrate payment gateways on their website or app to make sure their customers experience hassle-free payment transactions. The technology platform is PCI DSS complaint, making it highly secure, reliable and safe for digital transactions.

How much does Easebuzz payment gateway cost?

Easebuzz offers customisable pricing structure basis your business needs. Please connect with your relationship manager for more details.

How long will it take to begin accepting card payments?

Once the payment gateway integration is done in your system, it will start accepting card payments within a day. Payment gateway allows your customers to pay by credit card, debit card, 50+ net banking options, wallets like OLA Money, UPI and EMI.

What does it mean to be PCI compliant?

The Payment Card Industry (PCI) applies to companies of any size that accept credit card payments. It refers to the technical and operational standards that businesses follow to secure and protect credit card data provided by cardholders and transmitted through card processing transactions. When your payment gateway is PCI DSS complaint, the transactions made on the platform is safe and secure.

Will I be able to accept payments via Netbanking?

Yes, you can accept payments via Netbanking. Easebuzz payment gateway allows you to accept payments online with 50+ Netbanking options like HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, AXIS Bank, YES Bank, State Bank of India etc.

Why should I give my KYC documents to Easebuzz?

In the process of integrating payment gateway in your system, KYC document is required as the details are captured via Easebuzz payments bank’s authorized application and are transmitted securely to our servers. These data are not stored in any representative’s handset. Our process follows strict regulatory guidelines laid down for banks and are frequently subjected to external audits.

Do I need a website to accept Payment Online?

If you already have a website you can directly integrate Easebuzz payment gateway. If not, collect payments without website using Payment Links from Easebuzz merchant dashboard and easily share the links with customers via Email / SMS / WhatsApp and start accepting payments online.

How do I pay transaction fee to Easebuzz?

We have the lowest transaction charges in the industry. When integrating payment gateway for the website in India, Easebuzz collects transaction fees from its customers on payments received towards the invoices raised. Take for instance, you are a GST registered business owner and you collect payments from your customers via Easebuzz. You raise an invoice for ₹10000 and Easebuzz charges you a transaction fee of ₹100 (1%) and 18% GST on the fees. The amount received would be the balance after Easebuzz deducts the transaction fees. Steps on how to integrate payment gateway on website

  • Integrating a payment gateway on a website is a crucial process that enables businesses to accept online payments securely and efficiently. Here are the steps to successfully integrate a payment gateway:

    • Steps on How to Integrate Payment Gateway on Website
  • 1. Choose a Payment Gateway
  • 2. Create an Account
  • 3. Obtain API Credentials
  • 4. Review API Documentation
  • 5. Develop or Customise Integration
  • 6. Set up Test Environment
  • 7. Implement Payment Flow
  • 8. Handle Call-backs and Notifications
  • 9. Test and Debug
  • 10. Security and Compliance
  • 11. Go Live
  • 12. Monitor and Maintain

  • 1. Choose a payment gateway : Select a reliable payment gateway that aligns with your business needs and supports the preferred payment methods for your customers.

  • 2. Create an account : Sign up for an account with the chosen payment gateway provider. Provide the necessary details and complete the registration process.

  • 3. Obtain API credentials : After creating an account, obtain the API credentials (e.g., API keys, merchant ID) required to connect your website to the payment gateway.

  • 4. Review API documentation : Familiarize yourself with the payment gateway’s API documentation to understand how to send and receive payment data.

  • 5. Develop or customise integration : Depending on your website’s platform and requirements, develop a custom integration or use pre-built plugins provided by the payment gateway.

  • 6. Set up test environment : Create a testing environment to simulate transactions without processing real payments. This allows you to identify and fix any issues before going live.

  • 7. Implement payment flow : Integrate the payment flow into your website’s checkout process, ensuring a seamless and user-friendly experience for customers.

  • 8. Handle call-backs and notifications : Set up call-backs or webhooks to receive transaction notifications from the payment gateway, enabling real-time updates on payment status.

  • 9. Test and debug : Thoroughly test the payment gateway integration to check for errors or glitches. Debug and resolve any issues that may arise during testing.

  • 10. Security and compliance : Prioritize security measures to safeguard customer data and comply with industry standards and regulations, such as PCI DSS.

  • 11. Go Live : Once all tests are successful and you are confident in the integration, go live and start accepting actual payments on your website.

  • 12. Monitor and maintain : Regularly monitor the payment gateway’s performance and maintain the integration to ensure smooth and uninterrupted payment processing for your customers.

  • By following these steps, businesses can seamlessly integrate a payment gateway into their website, enabling smooth and secure online transactions, enhancing customer satisfaction and driving business growth.

Types of Payment Gateway available

  • 1. UPI payment gateway : Easebuzz UPI payment gateway offers businesses multiple UPI solutions to collect payments directly into bank accounts without any hassles. It comes with SDKs and APIs along with detailed API documentation.
  • 2. Hosted payment gateway : Easebuzz is a popular hosted payment gateway. It is easy for the business to setup, user-friendly and comes with high security with PCI DSS compliance. While the customer makes his purchase, he is redirected to the host page and after filling payment details redirects back to the business page to complete the sale. As the hosted payment gateway is external, businesses will not have complete control over checkout experience.
  • 3. Self-hosted payment gateway : Self-hosted payment gateway enables a faster checkout process. If businesses want to have control over user experience self-hosted checkout payment gateway is the best option. The details of transaction are completed encrypted and are shared with third-party gateway for authorisation.
  • 4. API hosted payment gateway : API hosted Payment gateway refers to technical interface that enables businesses to integrate payment gateway services into their applications or websites. Easebuzz API hosted payment gateway provides developers with the tools they need to build a secure and reliable payment processing system that can accept a variety of payment methods and manage payment transactions.
