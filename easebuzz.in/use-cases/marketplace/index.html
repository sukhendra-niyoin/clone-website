E-commerce payment system to power your marketplace with easy payment solutions

Solving financial complexities of marketplace platform

How we simplify payment solution for e-commerce

Make onboarding, moving funds and payouts easier with a fully compliant, global and flexible payment solution for marketplaces. Easebuzz is a simple and scalable online payment platform that offers payment API for marketplace platforms.

Merchant services by Easebuzz

Many of the marketplace platforms want to have a system through which they can manage the entire payment cycle which is backbreaking. This includes logistics credits work, payments acceptance, settlement flow between the retailer and customer. In medium and large enterprises, onboarding a new supplier or vendor tends to be a major problem. They contend with an outdated approach to supplier onboarding that involves broken communication chains, unnecessary delays, and error-prone manual tasks. This accompanies the transparency issue that relates to the overall vendor onboarding process along with various sub-processes.

Easebuzz helps businesses contend with these challenges. It aids to transform supplier and vendor onboarding into a modernized, more efficient process that alleviates the burden of employees and vendors alike. It allows marketplaces to configure vendor payouts, track balances, and reconcile vendor payments with vendor payouts from one API. Similarly, major issues towards making payments to vendors from a single dashboard, split payments to multiple sellers, settling funds, and deducting their commissions is solved by Easebuzz.

Helping Marketplaces ease collections and payouts

Incentivised solutions to help marketplace digitize their transactions.
Easebuzz has a unique software solution created on top of payment collection and payouts, designed for Marketplaces to offer core financial and ancillary services to help firms digitize their business transactions, collections, and settlements with ease.

We will manage your vendor payments so you don't have to worry about the payouts

Businesses can manage multiple vendors from one place and settle funds. They can make sure that all KYC compliances are met and that GST procedures are followed without difficulty.

Instant settlement on the same day

All transactions are processed in real-time in a virtual merchant account. You can deposit this directly into your bank account every day without waiting.

In minutes, you can start e-invoicing and processing digital payments

Automate your billing process to reduce the time and effort required for reconciliation and follow-up. You can personalize emails and SMS reminders to meet your needs.

Recurring payments solution with partial or full payments, as per your requirements

Invoicing system with GST compliant invoices for your customers. This can be added to a payment link solution, which could be shared with customers via email or SMS.

Auto reconciliation with your ERP and CRM

Our automated Vendor reconciliation process will ensure that you don't miss any invoices or credits.

Automatically Split and Settle Funds

Can quickly reconcile, split, and settle your payments automatically to split payments to different vendors in minutes. Our Robust dashboard helps you to keep track of your balances, batch earnings, and payouts. It can also transfer funds to vendors with tailoring options for price and commission split. Appropriate for businesses that have multiple vendors and do not have a Nodal account.
